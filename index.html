Forensic Assessment Case Study: Expenditure Anomalies

Focus: Corporate Procurement & Risk Management

This report presents findings from a forensic data assessment of departmental expenditure. The primary objective was to demonstrate the identification of internal control weaknesses and significant financial outliers within a high-volume procurement environment.

Expert Forensic Recommendations

To mitigate the internal control vulnerabilities exposed by this variance data, the following mitigation protocols are recommended:

  • Multi-Signature Mandate: Enforce mandatory dual-authorization tracking for all corporate vendor payouts exceeding Ksh 500,000.
  • Log Reconciliation: Cross-verify transaction TR-1620 retroactively against physical gate entry logs and validated delivery notes.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Deploy automated dashboard flag alerts inside the accounting workflow to catch variance spikes before payment dispatch.
